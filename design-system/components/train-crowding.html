A realistic EMU car, recoloured live by how packed it is — 1 (empty) → 5 (full)
How it works: every train on the map is drawn in its crowding colour, updated as it picks up and drops riders along the line. A run of red (4–5) trains means a corridor is over capacity — add trains (fleet) or a parallel line. The rail underneath keeps the line's own colour, so identity + load both read at a glance.
